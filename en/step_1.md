### Hardware

+ A Raspberry Pi computer
+ A buzzer
+ 5 × pipe cleaners
+ 2 × M-F jumper wires
+ 2 × F-F jumper wires
+ 2 × crocodile clip wires
+ Aluminium foil
+ Plasticine or BluTack

### Software

+ Scratch 3 Desktop
